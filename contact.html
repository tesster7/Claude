FAQ

Common Questions

Is KoboToolbox really free for nonprofits?

Yes, completely. KoboToolbox is provided free of charge to all nonprofit organizations. Kobo's mission is to ensure every organization working for social good has access to high-quality data tools, regardless of budget.

How do I get technical support for KoboToolbox?

For technical questions, visit the Community Forum or Support Center. These resources have answers to thousands of questions and an active community of users.

How can my organization partner with Kobo?

We welcome partnerships with humanitarian organizations, research institutions, and technology providers. Use the contact form above with "Partnership Inquiry" as the subject, and our team will be in touch.

Can I support Kobo financially?

Yes! As a nonprofit, Kobo relies on funding from donors and foundations to maintain free access to KoboToolbox for nonprofits worldwide. Contact us to discuss donation or funding opportunities.

What is the Ambassadors Program?

The KoboToolbox Ambassadors Program is a global network of volunteers who support organizations in their local communities to solve challenges using data-informed approaches. Learn more on our Our Work page.
