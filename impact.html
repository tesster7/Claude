Impact Stories

Real Change, Real People

From refugee camps to climate monitoring stations, see how organizations are using Kobo to transform how they work.

🏕️
Humanitarian

UNHCR Transforms Refugee Registration in East Africa

UNHCR uses KoboToolbox to register and track millions of refugees across East Africa. By shifting from paper-based to digital data collection, field teams cut data processing time by 70% and improved the accuracy of aid targeting — getting assistance to the right people faster.

🏥
Development

MSF Monitors Disease Outbreaks in Real Time

Médecins Sans Frontières uses KoboToolbox to track disease outbreaks and monitor patient outcomes in remote communities. Offline data collection capabilities are critical in areas without internet connectivity, enabling teams to gather data and sync when connection is available.

🌍
Environment

Mapping Climate Displacement Across the Pacific

Environmental researchers use Kobo's mobile tools to document the human impact of rising sea levels across Pacific island communities. The data collected directly informs international climate policy and adaptation planning, giving local voices a platform in global conversations.

Platform Impact

KoboToolbox by the Numbers

The scale of data collection happening through KoboToolbox every day reflects the global demand for accessible, open-source data tools.

📋
Millions of Forms Submitted

Organizations submit tens of millions of data collection forms every year through KoboToolbox — from household surveys to medical assessments to environmental monitoring reports.

📱
Offline-First by Design

KoboToolbox is built to work without internet connectivity — critical for field teams working in remote and conflict-affected areas. Data syncs automatically when a connection is available.

🔒
Security & Privacy

We take data security seriously. KoboToolbox includes features for encrypting sensitive data, managing user permissions, and ensuring data sovereignty — essential for human rights and protection work.
