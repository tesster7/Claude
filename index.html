Our Mission

Empowering Organizations to Create Change Through Data

Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to generate insights and make informed decisions that drive positive change around the world. As the need for assistance continues to escalate globally, this mission has never been more critical.

Accessibility

What We Do

Four Core Initiatives Driving Our Mission

Kobo fulfills its mission through four interconnected initiatives that strengthen the global social impact ecosystem.

🛠️

KoboToolbox

An open-source data collection, management, and visualization platform widely used in crisis and humanitarian response. Intuitive, powerful, and reliable — used by 32,000+ organizations daily, completely free for nonprofits.

Learn more →
🎓

Kobo Academy

Kobo's official online learning platform offering high-quality, self-paced courses designed by our team of experts. Training and capacity-building programs to help users gain expertise in data tools and methodology.

Learn more →
💼

Kobo Services

Technical and strategic support including implementation, customization, and long-term data strategy guidance. A wide range of data services supporting a global community of purpose-driven organizations.

Learn more →
🔬

Kobo Research

Supporting organizations in using data for evaluation, planning, and advocacy through applied research partnerships. Actively leading data-driven research initiatives to advance evidence-based decision making.

Learn more →
