Who We Are

A Nonprofit Dedicated to Data for Good

Kobo is a nonprofit technology organization that creates and supports open-source tools and services to improve how data is collected, managed, and used in humanitarian, development, and human rights work.

We are registered in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA — but our team is based around the world. We believe that every organization working in humanitarian action, development, environmental protection, peacebuilding, and human rights should have access to high-quality data collection tools.

Our name "Kobo" comes from the Acholi word meaning "transfer" — reflecting our core purpose of transferring knowledge, capacity, and data tools to communities that need them most.

Mission

To Empower Organizations to Generate Insights and Drive Positive Change

Kobo's mission is to provide accessible, cutting-edge data technology to support data-driven change that benefits everyone. As the need for assistance continues to escalate globally, this mission has never been more critical.

Core Values

What Guides Everything We Do

Our values define how we build technology, support communities, and operate as an organization committed to positive social change.

♿

Accessibility

We maintain open-source tools that are intuitive and accessible to all organizations promoting social good. Providing quality tools to local actors and volunteer networks — often under-resourced — is key to our vision.

🤝

Respect & Inclusiveness

Dedicated to protecting the human rights, autonomy, and dignity of all persons — including our team members, partners and users, and the communities we serve around the world.

🔬

Innovation

We push the frontiers of data innovation with purpose-built, next-generation tools for positive social change and frontline response — where timely, accurate data can save lives.

🌐

Community-Centered

We build with communities, not just for them. Our global Ambassador network, open forums, and collaborative development ensure tools reflect real needs from the field.

🔓

Openness

All our core tools are open source. We believe transparency in technology is fundamental to trust, sustainability, and the long-term empowerment of the communities we serve.

📊

Accountability

We hold ourselves to the highest standards — earning a 4-star Charity Navigator rating and Candid Platinum Seal of Transparency. We are stewards of the trust placed in us.
