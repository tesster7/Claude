Overview

Advancing Equitable Data Solutions

Through our four core initiatives, Kobo strengthens the global social impact ecosystem — ensuring that social impact organizations have the tools, knowledge, and support they need to create lasting change through ethical, community-centered data practices.

Initiative 01

KoboToolbox

KoboToolbox is an open-source data collection, management, and visualization platform — widely recognized as the world's most popular data collection tool in challenging settings.

Intuitive, powerful, and reliable, KoboToolbox is used to collect, analyze, and manage data for surveys, monitoring, evaluation, and research across humanitarian, development, and social impact sectors.

Over 32,000 nonprofit organizations worldwide rely on KoboToolbox every day — and it is provided completely free of charge to nonprofits, giving projects around the world access to high-quality data tools.

Initiative 02

Kobo Academy

Kobo Academy is Kobo's official online learning platform, offering high-quality, self-paced courses designed by our team of experts. It provides training and capacity-building programs to help users gain expertise in data tools and methodology.

Through Kobo Academy, we democratize access to data expertise — ensuring that field workers, program managers, and data teams in any organization can develop the skills they need to use data effectively.

Our Ambassadors Program complements the Academy — a global network of volunteers who support organizations in their local communities, helping them solve challenges through data-informed initiatives.

Initiative 03

Kobo Services

Launched in 2024, Kobo Partner Services provides expert data support for nonprofits. Our team of specialists works alongside organizations to deliver technical and strategic guidance across the data lifecycle.

From custom server deployments to full-scale data strategy, Kobo Services bridges the gap between powerful tools and organizational capacity — helping teams use data more effectively.

We also offer custom server solutions — setting up and maintaining secure, standalone, custom-branded KoboToolbox servers for organizations that need dedicated infrastructure.

Initiative 04

Kobo Research

Officially launched in 2025, Kobo Research supports organizations in using data for evaluation, planning, and advocacy through applied research partnerships.

We actively support and lead data-driven research initiatives, drawing on nearly two decades of experience in humanitarian data collection to help organizations generate insights that matter.

Kobo Research ensures that the data collected through KoboToolbox translates into meaningful evidence — informing decisions, improving programs, and advocating for change at all levels.
